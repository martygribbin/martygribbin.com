About The Project

H2Flow Water Management is a water utility company based in Armagh with a presence all over the island of Ireland. They required a business website to showcase their unique services they would offer.

The final build was a responsive website where their business gains leads from Belfast, Dublin and Mayo, the three main areas they would be competing.
